\chapter{Experiment Two}
\label{chp6}

Chapter \ref{chp6} will cover the concept of 'high frequency dynamics'. High frequency dynamics in this context are the faster dynamics in comparison to the mechanical models in the Motorlab and NERMLAB systems. In experiment 2 the higher frequency or faster dynamics are a low pass filter on the output speed from the nominal plant\footnote{Nominal plant being the combination of the mechanical dynamics and derivative, as seen in figure \ref{SPEEDCSZOOM}}.
